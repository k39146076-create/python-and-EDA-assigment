Q 5.WHAT YOU LEARNED ABOUT EDA AND DATA ANALYSIS?
Through this project, I learned that Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is an iterative, detective-like process. It is not just about writing clean code or generating pretty charts, but about asking deeper questions when patterns emerge. I realized how critical data cleaning, understanding data types (continuous vs. discrete), and checking distributions are before drawing conclusions. EDA bridges raw, chaotic numbers into clear, actionable business strategies.
